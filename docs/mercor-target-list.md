# Fintech VC/PE/Accelerator Target List for Code/Operational Data Licensing

*Research compiled on March 3, 2026*

## Executive Summary

This list contains 25 fintech-focused venture capital firms, private equity firms, accelerators, and corporate venture arms whose portfolio companies would be ideal targets for code/operational data licensing opportunities. Focus is on firms with large portfolios in B2B fintech, financial infrastructure, and insurtech where companies typically have substantial Python/TypeScript codebases with 10+ engineers.

**Priority Ranking System:**
- **Priority 1 (Highest)**: Large portfolios (50+ companies), strong B2B fintech focus, proven value for standardized due diligence
- **Priority 2 (Medium)**: Mid-size portfolios (20-50 companies), some B2B fintech focus, potential licensing targets
- **Priority 3 (Lower)**: Smaller portfolios or primarily consumer-focused, but still relevant targets
