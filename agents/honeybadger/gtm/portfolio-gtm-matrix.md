## 1. ForwardLane / Signal Studio

### ICP
**Primary:** Head of Advisor Technology / Chief Digital Officer at RIAs, wirehouses, and asset managers (AUM $5B–$500B)
**Secondary:** Enterprise fintech vendor partnerships (platforms like Orion, Envestnet, Riskalyze)
**Proof of ICP:** Invesco is a $1.5T AUM asset manager — this validates the enterprise segment.

- Advisors spend 60%+ of time on data retrieval vs client-facing work
- Compliance teams need audit trails for investment decisions

| Practifi | CRM, no AI signals | Different category entirely |
| FP Alpha | Planning-centric, not signal-centric | Complementary, not competitive |

### Where ICP Hangs Out

### Channels (Priority Order)

2. **Partnership channels** — integrate with Orion/Envestnet to get distribution

### Positioning Statement
"Signal Studio gives financial advisors the ability to ask any question about their book of business in plain English — and get an answer in seconds. No SQL. No engineers. Just intelligence."

### Key Numbers to Lead With

## 3. Ultrafone
**AI Phone Receptionist + Social Engineering Detection**

### Status: 75% complete — NOT ready for broad GTM. Focus on design partners.

### What It Does
AI-powered phone receptionist that handles inbound calls AND detects social engineering attempts (vishing, pretexting, fraud calls). Dual value prop: efficiency + security.

### ICP
**Primary (receptionist play):** SMBs with high call volume — dental/medical offices, law firms, real estate agencies, home services
**Primary (security play):** Financial advisors, wealth management firms, banks — social engineering is a massive compliance/fraud risk
**Sweet spot:** RIAs and financial services firms (connects to ForwardLane network)

### Pain Points We Solve
- Dental/law offices: receptionists cost $40K+/year, after-hours calls go unanswered
- Financial firms: SEC/FINRA increasingly citing social engineering as top fraud vector
- "Grandparent scams" targeting elderly clients = liability for advisors

### Competitors
| Competitor | Weakness | Our Angle |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| Ruby Receptionist | Human-staffed, expensive ($500+/mo) | AI-native, fraction of cost |
| Smith.ai | Chat-focused, phone is secondary | Phone-first |
| Google CCAI | Enterprise complexity | SMB-friendly |
| No direct competitor | Social engineering detection is NEW | First mover in this specific niche |

### GTM Strategy (Pre-Launch)
1. **Find 3-5 design partners** — ideally financial advisors (cross-sell from ForwardLane network)
2. **Document everything** — video the product working, capture testimonials BEFORE launch
3. **Pre-launch waitlist** — simple landing page, collect emails now
4. **Target niche first** — pick ONE vertical (RIAs or dental), dominate it, then expand

### Channels
1. **ForwardLane network** — warm intro to advisors already in the fold
2. **LinkedIn** — security angle gets traction ("Your phone is being socially engineered right now")
3. **Direct outreach** — advisor compliance officers at RIAs

### Positioning Statement
"Ultrafone is the first AI receptionist that doesn't just answer your phones — it protects your clients from fraud while it does."
