# LinkedIn GTM Playbook — Paolo Trivellato (March 2026)

Source: X thread, $950K added to clients' MRR with organic LinkedIn content.

## Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Profile Architecture
- Profile = landing page, NOT resume
- **Headshot**: Professional, real face, trust signal
- **Headline formula**: `[outcome] for [ICP] using [mechanism]`
  - e.g. "I help B2B SaaS founders add $500k-2M ARR through organic LinkedIn content"
- **Banner**: Core offer + proof (client logos, revenue numbers, metrics)
- **About section**: 4 paragraphs — background → transition → who you help → soft CTA (DM trigger word)
- **Featured section**: CTA ladder — high/medium/low friction offers

### Pillar 2: Content Engine (3 formats)
1. **Text posts** (3-5/week) — Authority builder. Hook → Body → Close+CTA. 300-1000 words.
   - Hook patterns: contrarian claim, surprising data, personal story with contrast
   - One sentence per line, space between paragraphs, bold key insights
2. **Short caption + video** (2-3/week) — Trust builder. Curiosity caption → 2-10 min video.
   - Video-nurtured leads close at 60-70% vs text-only 20-30%
3. **Short text + graphic** (2-3/week) — Viral amplifier. Bold opinion caption → carousel/infographic.

### Pillar 3: Distribution (Multi-Account)
- **Tier 1**: Founder/CEO — daily, thought leadership (50K-500K followers)
- **Tier 2**: Co-founders — 3-5x/week, tactical expertise (10K-50K)
- **Tier 3**: Team members — 1-3x/week, behind-scenes (1K-10K)
- Cross-amplification: all accounts engage within first hour → algorithm flywheel
- Employee advocacy: 10 employees = 10x organic reach, zero cost

## Three Missing Pieces

1. **Lead magnet posts** — Weekly giveaway posts. 1 post = 1000-2000 comments, 80% email capture, 50-100 DMs. Can generate $50K-200K/month.
2. **Value-based case studies** — Situation → Diagnosis → Solution → Transformation → Lesson → Soft CTA
3. **Featured section CTA ladder** — High friction (book call) + Medium (download guide) + Low (newsletter). Capture at every awareness stage.

## Key Metrics
- Cold leads: 2-5% close rate, pay $1K
- Warm LinkedIn leads: 30-50% close rate, pay $10K (same service, 10x price)
- Video-nurtured: 60-70% close rate
- Single lead magnet post: 200-400 profile views/day for a week
- 90 daily posts = 90 touchpoints vs cold outreach's 3-5

## Core Principle: Value-First
- Trust before transaction
- Positioning as source, not salesman
- Goodwill loop (law of reciprocity)
- Content as proof of expertise
- Staying top-of-mind without being pushy
