### 1. 🚨 We Surface At-Risk AUM Before It Walks Out the Door

ForwardLane monitors every advisor relationship for the early warning signs of defection: declining AUM, reduced wholesaler contact frequency, competitive fund exposure, and engagement drop-off. When the pattern matches, the signal fires — and your wholesaler gets the alert *before* the advisor has made a decision. In a volatile rate environment, this early warning system is the difference between a proactive conversation and a redemption letter.

> **The math:** If Signal Studio protects even 0.1% of AUM under management from churning, at Invesco's scale that's $1.5 billion in retained assets. At your average management fee, that's tens of millions in protected revenue — per year.
