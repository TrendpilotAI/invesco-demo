## 🔥 The Hidden Gem Nobody's Talking About

### 3. Ultrafone
**What it is:** An AI phone receptionist built on **Pipecat + Groq + Fish Audio + Deepgram**. Python backend (FastAPI), React frontend. Full-stack.

**Why this is secretly the most interesting project:**
- **Real-time AI call screening** with Groq (llama-3.1-70b) for conversation
- **Security analyzer** that detects social engineering in real-time — urgency manipulation, authority impersonation, information fishing, financial scams — with 60+ pattern rules + LLM analysis
- **Call classifier** that routes healthcare, ForwardLane, known contacts, and unknown callers differently
- **Fish Audio TTS** for voice responses (your cheapest TTS option at $0.015/min)
- **Supabase backend** with proper migrations, Redis caching
- **Frontend:** Dashboard, Call History, Live Call, Contacts, Settings, Connections pages
- **Railway deployment ready** (Dockerfile + railway.toml + nixpacks)
- **PostHog analytics** integrated

**The kicker:** This is a CONSUMER PRODUCT. Everyone hates robocalls. AI call screening is a billion-dollar market (see: Truecaller, Hiya, Google Call Screen). But Nathan built something WAY more sophisticated — it's not just screening, it's an AI *receptionist* that handles calls intelligently.

**Revenue potential if shipped:** Subscription SaaS, $5-15/mo per user. Market size: Everyone with a phone.
